What I've Learned in this Course

Internet is essentially a global network made up of billions of computers. These billions of computers
communicate with each other which allow people to share information in so many different ways! The
internet operates through these physical cables that even wireless connections rely on in order to access the global network.

World Wide Web is all the public websites or pages that anyone can have access to from their own or local
computers through the internet. Pages and various documents are interconnected using hyperlinks that users will
click on to find more information. This information includes things like; text, audio, video, and images.
